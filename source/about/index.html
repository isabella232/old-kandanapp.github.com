About Us


We love team communication tools and have used them for years, just like many other developers out there. Currently setting up a team communication server takes time, resources and a custom solution to achieve some of the most basic features like message archiving. This is why Kandan was built.

Kandan was originally developed by the team over at Cloudfuji and they started development on it back in March of 2012. The current development team took over Kandan in January of 2013.
