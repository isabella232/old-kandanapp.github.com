Full Emoji Support

Full support for the Emoji emoticons set.

Emoji (絵文字, or えもじ?) (pronounced eh-moh-jee) is the Japanese term for the picture characters or emoticons used in Japanese electronic messages and webpages. Originally meaning pictograph, the word literally means "picture" (e) + "letter" (moji).
